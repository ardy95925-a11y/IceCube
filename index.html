Server Rules
Please read and follow all rules to keep our community safe
1
Be Respectful
Treat all members with kindness and respect. Harassment, hate speech, slurs, or personal attacks of any kind are not tolerated.
Ban
2
No Spam
Do not flood channels with repeated messages, emojis, or meaningless content. Keep conversations on-topic for each channel.
Warn
3
No NSFW Content
NSFW (Not Safe For Work) content is strictly forbidden outside of designated age-restricted channels (if any exist).
Ban
4
No Self-Promotion / Advertising
Do not advertise other Discord servers, YouTube channels, social media, or any personal projects without prior approval from staff.
Mute
5
Follow Discord's ToS
All members must be 13+ years of age and comply with Discord's Terms of Service at all times.
Required
6
Listen to Staff
Moderator and Admin decisions are final. If you have a concern, open a ticket or DM a staff member — don't argue publicly.
Warn

Role Guide
Understand what each role can do and how to earn them
👑 Admin
Server owners and administrators. They manage the server structure, bots, and major decisions.
Manage Server Ban Members Manage Roles
🛡️ Moderator
Trusted members who enforce rules, handle reports, and keep the server clean and friendly.
Kick Members Manage Messages Timeout
⭐ Veteran
Long-standing members who have been active for a long time and earned community trust.
Special Channels Custom Color
🤖 Bot
Automated bots that provide utility, music, moderation, and fun features.
Auto-assigned
👋 Member
Every verified member of the server. Default role given after agreeing to the rules.
Read Channels Send Messages
🎉 Event Host
Members who are trusted to host and manage community events and giveaways.
Host Events Ping Members
